**Mission Control** is a [group house](/group-housing) in the Mission District neighborhood of San Francisco.

## Background

Mission Control was founded in 2013 by a group of Thiel Fellows. While it is no longer associated with the Thiel Fellowship, the house continues to be composed largely of people in SF's startup ecosystem[^1]
