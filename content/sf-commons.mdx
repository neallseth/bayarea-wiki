The Commons is a social space (often referred to as a third or fourth space[^1]) in Hayes Valley, founded by Patricia Mou and Adi Melamed.

## Background

The Commons first opened to the public in September of 2022. Since then, it has seen its membership grow to 500 members, most of whom belong to San Francisco's ["Megascene"](/megascene).
